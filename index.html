About Me

My name is Enrique Rosales, but usually people call me Ricky. I am 22 years old and I'm currently in a certificate program at Northwestern Univeristy.


A little backstory on me, I was born in Naperville, Illinois and grew up in a small townhouse in Aurora. I lived there until 3rd grade until I moved to Oak Park, Illinois where I finished elementary and most of middle school. I left Oak Park in 8th grade and moved to Yorkville, Illinois where I finsihed up middle school and went to high school until Sophomore year. Sophomore year I left Yorkville and moved to Warrenville, Illinois and finished my high school.


From high school I moved around for a bit until I moved to Park Ridge and decided to work in a butcher shop to maybe to decide to become a butcher. I worked there for a couple of years until I decided it wasn't for me. Working with computers have always been pretty easy for me (my Dad works with computers so I was exposed to them through him) so I saw an opportunity with this program and here I am today!
